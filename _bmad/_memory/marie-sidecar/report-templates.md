## Template Library

### Technical Report Template

**Sections:**
1. Executive Summary (1 page)
2. Problem Statement & Objectives
3. Data Sources & Preparation
4. Exploratory Analysis
5. Methodology & Approach
6. Results & Interpretation
7. Model Performance & Validation
8. Limitations & Assumptions
9. Recommendations
10. Appendix (code, additional plots)

**Format:** Quarto document (.qmd)
**Length:** 30-50 pages
**Audience:** Technical stakeholders, data science teams

### Executive Report Template

**Sections:**
1. Executive Summary (key findings + recommendations)
2. Business Impact
3. Top 3 Insights (visual-first)
4. Recommended Actions
5. Next Steps & Timeline

**Format:** Quarto document (.qmd) or slides
**Length:** 2-3 pages max
**Audience:** C-suite, business stakeholders

---

## Style Guidelines

**Technical Reports:**
- Code chunks shown with reproducible examples
- Statistical rigor with p-values, confidence intervals
- Methodology section explains choices
- Full data lineage documented

**Executive Reports:**
- No code visible
- Business language, minimal jargon
- Visual-first (plots > tables)
- Action-oriented recommendations
