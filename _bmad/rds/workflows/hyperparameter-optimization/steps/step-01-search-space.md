## PARAMETER RANGE GUIDANCE

**General Principles:**

1. **Start Wide** — Err on the side of exploration
2. **Use Transformations** — Log scale for exponential parameters (learning rate, regularization)
3. **Consider Interactions** — Some parameters depend on others (max_depth vs. min_n)
4. **Respect Constraints** — Dataset size limits (e.g., max_depth can't exceed log2(n_obs))
5. **Be Pragmatic** — Balance thoroughness with time

**Common Parameter Types:**

- **Learning Rates** — Log scale, 0.001 to 0.3
- **Regularization** — Log scale, 0.0001 to 10
- **Tree Depth** — Linear scale, 1 to 15
- **Ensemble Size** — Linear scale, 50 to 2000
- **Dropout Rates** — Linear scale, 0 to 0.5
